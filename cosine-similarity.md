# 🟢 Cosine Similarity

* <mark style="color:purple;background-color:purple;">**Measures angle between two vectors.**</mark>
* <mark style="color:purple;background-color:purple;">**Ignores magnitude.**</mark>
* Range: **-1 to 1**
* 1 → exactly same direction
* 0 → unrelated (orthogonal)
* -1 → opposite direction
