# Nodes

* <mark style="color:purple;background-color:purple;">**Generate (LLM):**</mark> Images + attributes + brand/category rules in the prompt; output title, bullets, long description.
* <mark style="color:purple;background-color:purple;">**Compliance (LLM): Validate against rules and attributes.**</mark>
* <mark style="color:purple;background-color:purple;">**Quality (LLM): Score dimensions and give feedback. ⇒ Scores across clarity, completeness, readability, brand alignment**</mark>
* <mark style="color:purple;background-color:purple;">**Decision (LLM):**</mark> APPROVE or FLAG\_FOR\_REVIEW from compliance + quality.
